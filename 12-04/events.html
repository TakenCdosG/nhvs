Clubhouse Conference Room

For small meetings the Clubhouse offers a 20' x 31', 640 square foot second floor meeting room. This may be reserved for the day or longer if needed.

The room contains 6' x 8' tables. Seating capabilities differ based on setup: classroom style allows for 20 people; theatre style allows for 30 people; U-shape allows for 15 people; and conference style allows for 20 people.

The following audio/visual equipment is available: high-speed internet access, speaker phone, screen, and flip chart. Catering services are also available upon request.
