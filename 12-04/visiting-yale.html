Living With Us

If your plan is to look for an apartment in town or stay in New Haven for a short while, we offer student and faculty all-inclusive living options at our hotel. There is no application, security deposit, or utility hook up fees. You have the flexibility to stay for as long as you want. NHVS has been the primary choice for many Yale visiting Fellows, research faculty, and also graduate students looking for flexible living arrangements in a tranquil, spacious, and convenient setting. You will enjoy free WiFi, HD cable, free shuttle service to campus, and daily hot breakfast and dinner Monday through Wednesday.
