Visiting Yale?

Tranquil. Spacious. Convenient.

New Haven Village Suites is an extended stay hotel, designed for people looking for flexibility and ease of living. Our community offers the conveniences of a hotel with the comforts of home. Whether you are traveling for business or pleasure, a day, a week, a month or a year, we will provide you with everything you need for a pleasant stay.

Directions & Transportation

A great alternative to the busy bustling streets of downtown New Haven.

If you decide to drive or rent a car, Premiere Hotel and Village Suites offers free on-site parking for our New Haven hotel guests conveniently located right outside your front door! New Haven Suites is located off of Route 95 tucked in a village-like setting about a mile from downtown New Haven. Our New Haven hotel is only two stories so don’t look up high.

Directions from I-95 North

From I-95 North – Exit 46. Turn right onto Long Wharf Drive
Proceed straight through intersection of Long Wharf Drive and Sargent Drive
Follow Long Wharf Drive to the end.

Directions from I-95 South

From I-95 South- Exit 46. Take a left at the light onto Sargent Drive
At the 1st light take a right onto Long Wharf Drive
Follow Long Wharf Drive to the end.

Directions from I-91 South

From I-91 South- (In New Haven I-91 South ends and merges with I-95)
Follow signs to I-95 South
Take Exit 46
Take a left at the light onto Sargent Drive
At the 1st light take a right onto Long Wharf Drive
Follow Long Wharf Drive to the end.

Directions from Tweed-New Haven Airport

Travel Northeast on Burr Street toward Fort Hale Road
Turn left onto Fort Hale Road
Turn right onto Townsend Avenue (CT-337)
Turn left onto Main Street and merge onto I-95 South/Governor John Davis Lodge Turnpike
Take the Sargent Drive exit- EXIT 46- toward Long Wharf and follow to end.

Transportation available from Tweed New Haven International Airport:
